Ahïa Solutions LLC is a company started in 2018 to find useful marketplace solutions for minority and Black owned businesses in the Research Triangle Park area. In 2018 the primary focus of the business has been to provide Marketing, Process, and Implementation services.

Since 2018, the goals of the business have shifted to wanting to be a one-stop-shop for web development, marketing, accounting, loans, investment, education, back office support, and recruitment.

At the end of the day, the goal of Ahïa Solutions is to DO WELL by DOING GOOD for the community.
